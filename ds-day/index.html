DS Day is a day when people play with their old DS/GameBoy-family systems. It's a time to have fun, relive positive memories, and experience the hyper-specific joys of these old handhelds.

When is it? Well, there isn't necessarily any one DS Day. You can see if there's one you can join in on or arrange your own! You have to get some people to commit to it, though. Personally, I might announce it to a local but sizeable community where a lot of people are likely to own one of these systems, like a school or city.

The exact parameters may vary—location, timing, allowed devices… but the core idea is to have fun with old handheld gaming systems in the modern day.

This site provides resources to help you arrange and/or have as much fun as possible on DS Day!
